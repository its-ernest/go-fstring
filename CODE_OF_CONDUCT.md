# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment or intimidation in any form
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Public or private abuse of project maintainers

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code of conduct.  
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers.
